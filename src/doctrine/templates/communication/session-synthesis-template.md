# Session Synthesis: [Session Title]

> **Purpose:** Convert raw session output — notes, transcripts, whiteboard captures,
> sticky clusters — into a structured artifact that can be acted on without attending
> the session.
>
> **When to use:** After any facilitated session (workshop, retrospective, alignment
> meeting, working group) where decisions, actions, or insights were produced.
>
> **Produced by:** Facilitator Felix (preparation and synthesis mode), or any agent
> tasked with post-session processing.
